What these mean for you
Butyrate 23 — gut lining under-fueled
Drives systemic inflammation, water retention, and immune activation. Sodium butyrate supplementation and prebiotic fiber (acacia, PHGG) are the most direct fixes.
Putrescine 12 — protein fermenting, not absorbing
Undigested protein fermenting in the colon causes lower abdominal distention. Take digestive enzymes with every protein-containing meal — not just breakfast.
TMA 26 + Uric Acid 17 — metabolic load
TMA converts to TMAO — a cardiovascular inflammatory compound. As a pescatarian, shellfish and excess eggs are your highest-choline foods. Rotate sardines and salmon as primary protein instead.
Methane 33 — slow motility + distention
Methane archaea slow gut transit, creating gas and bloat that mimics fat in the lower belly. Atrantil targets archaea directly. A lactulose SIBO breath test would confirm and quantify this.
LPS 89 — one genuine bright spot
No heavy pathogenic overgrowth. Your immune activation is from fermentation byproducts and gut permeability — not active infection. This means dietary and supplement interventions should be effective.
